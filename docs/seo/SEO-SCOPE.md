## 1. Business Overview

Jones Pressure Washing is an owner-operated exterior cleaning business serving Bergen County and Essex County, New Jersey. Founded in 2023 by Kyle Jones (background in automotive detailing and electrical trades), the company offers pressure washing, soft washing, gutter cleaning, roof cleaning, and seasonal holiday lighting.

The business differentiates on quality over speed — specialized equipment for each task, attention to detail, and a walk-around inspection guarantee. Fully insured, entering its 3rd season.

**Core Services:**
- Pressure washing (driveways, patios, concrete, pavers, wood restoration)
- Soft washing (siding, roofs, delicate surfaces)
- Gutter cleaning (debris removal, downspout flush, pipe jetting)
- Roof cleaning (soft wash method, moss/algae/black streak removal)
- Holiday lighting (seasonal, Oct-Jan)

## 3. Target Audience

**Primary:** Homeowners in Bergen & Essex County, NJ
- Own single-family homes or townhomes
- Notice dirty siding, driveways, or roofs
- May have received insurance notice about roof moss
- Value quality and reliability over lowest price

**Secondary:** Small business owners / commercial property managers
- Storefronts, sidewalks, loading zones
- Need regular maintenance (monthly/quarterly)
