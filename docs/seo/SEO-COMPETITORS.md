### Aggregator Competition
The biggest SEO competitors for local pressure washing searches are aggregator platforms:

## Content Gap Opportunities

Based on industry knowledge, these are common gaps in local pressure washing competitor sites:

### Content Jones PW Already Has (Competitive Advantage)
- Deep FAQ page with 20+ real Q&As (most competitors have 5-7 generic FAQs)
- Honest "What We Won't Pressure Wash" section (builds trust, rare in the industry)
- Gutter guard honest opinion with real client story
- Detailed explanation of soft wash vs pressure wash approach
- Surface-specific expertise sections (vinyl, stucco, roof types)

### Content Opportunities to Explore
- **Blog/articles**: Most local competitors don't blog consistently. Even 1-2 monthly posts on seasonal topics would build authority
- **Before/after gallery with context**: Not just photos, but descriptions of what was cleaned and the approach used
- **Pricing transparency**: Even a price range guide would capture "how much does pressure washing cost" searches
- **Video content**: Process videos showing actual work, equipment, and technique
- **Neighborhood-specific content**: Mentioning specific neighborhoods in city lander pages (e.g., "The Ridgewood Heights area" or "homes along Franklin Turnpike")

## Strategic Opportunities

### 1. FAQ Schema Advantage
Jones PW has a robust FAQPage schema with 20+ Q&As. Most competitors either don't have FAQ schema or have minimal coverage. This gives potential for rich snippets in search results.

### 2. Content Depth
The depth-of-value content from the client Q&A gives Jones PW significantly more expertise-signaling content than typical pressure washing sites. This aligns with Google's E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) signals.

### 3. City Lander Coverage
Dynamic city lander pages give broad local keyword coverage. Key is ensuring content quality — avoiding thin/templated pages that could be seen as doorway pages.

### 4. Service Differentiation Content
"What We Won't Wash" and honest gutter guard opinion differentiate from competitors who claim to do everything. This builds trust and can rank for informational queries.
